Welcome! Here you will learn about the different types of html forms and how they work.


Registration Form

A registration form is used when someone needs to sign up for something — like a course, event, website, or even a membership.

Purpose of a registration form:

  • To collect basic info from a person (name, email, etc.)
  • To let them select a course/program
  • To confirm if they agree to the terms

Survey Form

A survey form is a type of form used to collect people’s opinions, experiences, or feedback about something (a product, service, event, etc.).

Purpose of a survey form:

  • To gather feedback
  • To know people’s satisfaction level
  • To know what should be improved

Biodata Form

A biodata form (biographical data form) is used to collect a person’s basic personal information. It is often used for applications, sign-ups, school records, and club memberships.

Purpose of a biodata form:

  • To gather personal details
  • To collect contact information
  • To list interests or membership details
